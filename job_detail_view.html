BD & Comms Executive Assistant

Remote /
Communications /
Full-time

Ukiyox is a leading community-centric multi-chain NFT marketplace with over $90 million in trading volume to date and over 2 million monthly visitors. Ukiyox is home to a variety of PFP collections, digital art, music, metaverse lands, and much more.

Ukiyox has an extremely active community of followers, creators, curators and collectors who are actively shaping the future of our marketplace and the digital ownership in web3.

We are looking for an executive assistant for the BD and Marketing teams to support day-to-day operations, help structure and streamline work processes.

Join our motivated and rapidly growing global, enthusiastic and fully remote team to help build the #1 NFT marketplace of the future!

Key Responsibilities
  • Coordinating and scheduling internal and external calls
  • Structuring information, working with spreadsheets
  • Making notes and preparing meeting minutes
  • Assistance in communication with partners/third partiesAssistance in research and data compilation
Skills & Qualifications
  • High attention to detail, structured and punctual
  • Ability to structure and maintain effective processes
  • Communicative, team player
  • Experienced in working with GSuite tools
What do we offer
  • Working for a rapidly expanding global rocketship
  • Unique chance to contribute to the future of NFTs & web3
  • Team that cares about products and working conditions
  • Flexible hours
  • Full-time, paid vacations
  • Attractive packages, including healthcare and dental benefits
